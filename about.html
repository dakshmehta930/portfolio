🚀 Where It All Began

My journey into digital marketing started with a simple curiosity — why do some brands stick in your mind while others vanish? That question led me down a rabbit hole of marketing books, YouTube channels, and countless hours of experimentation. I started building skills not from a classroom, but from real curiosity and obsession with the craft.

💡 Learning by Doing

I didn't wait for the perfect moment. I started creating content, designing graphics, and building small projects. Every mistake taught me more than any course could. I learned Canva before Photoshop, WordPress before code, and social strategy before paid ads. Practical skills, built fast.

📍 First Real-World Impact — Philips Gallery Bathinda

Landing my first real client — Philips Gallery Bathinda — was a turning point. I managed their complete social media presence: content calendar, creatives, captions, and community engagement. Watching follower counts grow and seeing real people engage with content I created was the validation I needed to go all-in.

⚡ Building The Marketian

With real experience under my belt, I founded The Marketian — my own agency brand. I designed the full identity: logo, colour palette, brand voice, website, and social media. This wasn't just a business venture — it was proof of concept. If I could build a brand from zero, I could do it for clients too.

🤖 Embracing AI as a Superpower

When AI tools exploded onto the scene, I didn't see a threat — I saw a massive opportunity. I dove deep into ChatGPT, Claude, Gamma, and a dozen other tools. Today, I use AI to generate ideas faster, write better briefs, create content at scale, and deliver client results that would've taken twice as long before.
