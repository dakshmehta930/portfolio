FAQs

Common Questions

What's your typical project timeline? +

It depends on the scope. A social media package takes 3–5 days to set up. A WordPress website typically takes 1–3 weeks. Design packages can be delivered in 3–7 days. I'll always give you a clear timeline before we start.

Do you work with international clients? +

Absolutely. I work remotely with clients worldwide. Communication happens via email, WhatsApp, or video calls — whatever works best for you across time zones.

What information do you need to start? +

Just send me a message with a brief description of what you need, your business/brand, and your goals. I'll ask follow-up questions in our first call. No need to have everything figured out before reaching out.

How does payment work? +

I typically work with a 50% upfront deposit and 50% on completion for project-based work. Monthly retainers are billed at the start of each month. Payment methods are discussed during the onboarding process.
